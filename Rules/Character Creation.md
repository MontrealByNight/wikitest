# Character Creation

Following the Rulebook Character creation, with slight edits regarding the age of the Characters.

### Attributes
- Take one attribute at 4, three at 3, four at 2 and one at 1.

### Skills 
Pick one Skill distribution between Jack of all Trade, Balanced or Specialist:
- **Jack of all trades**: One Skill at 3; eight Skills at 2; ten Skills at 1.
- **Balanced**: Three Skills at 3; five Skills at 2; seven Skills at 1.
- **Specialist**: One Skill at 4; three Skills at 3; three Skills at 2; three Skills at 1.

*Add free specialties to Academics, Craft, Performance, and Science Skills. Take one more free specialty*.

### Disciplines
- Choose 2 disciplines of your in-clan pool. Take 2 points in one, 1 point in another.
- Caitiffs choose 2 discipline and do the same spread.
- Thin-Blood characters have no intrinsic Discipline.

### Humanity
- The humanity value of a character is noted at character creation and creates your base humanity pool.
- A PC that loses humanity can try to reacquire it at no exp cost up to your base humanity pool.
- Increasing the base pool requires paying experience (3x level).

### Experience (Weekly Earning)
- 1 XP for waking up the character and posting at least 3 different emotes in a week.
- 1 XP for interacting with another character.
- 1 XP for progressing personal or someone else's story.
- 1 XP for participating in STs led scenes.
- 1 XP can be assigned for extremely evil acts (3+ stains) or extremely heroic acts.
  
# Age and Ancillae Rules

### Age Categories
- **Childer or Fledgling**: Embraced within the last 15 years. 12th/13th generation, Blood Potency 1.
- **Neonates**: Embraced between 1940 and a decade ago. 12th/13th generation, Blood Potency 1. Receive +15 experience points.
- **Ancillae**: Embraced between 1780 and 1940. Total age not over 300 years. Generation as low as 10th, starting Blood Potency 2.

### Expanded Ancillae Rules (In Memoriam Update)
- **12th generation**: Blood Potency 1. Gain 8 dots of Advantages and 12 experience points to spend on skills.
- **11th or 10th generation**: Blood Potency 2. Gain 8 dots of Advantages and 3 dots of flaws.
- **9th or 8th generation**: Blood Potency 3. Gain 5 dots of flaws. May take 2 dots of Advantages at the cost of permanent loss of 1 Humanity.

### Oceans of Time (Eras)
Depending on your backstory and generation, choose your Eras. Cumulative age combined with other factors cannot bring Humanity lower than 4.
- **Up to 100 years ago**: Choose 3 Eras. Receive Flaws of 1 of those.
- **Up to 150 years ago**: Choose 4 Eras. Receive Flaws of 2 of those. Lose 1 permanent dot of Humanity.
- **Older than 150 years**: Choose 5 Eras. Receive Flaws of 3 of those. Lose 2 permanent dots of Humanity.

# Merits, Flaws, and Loresheets

### Backgrounds and Loresheets
- Every character has 7 points to distribute between Merits, Backgrounds and Loresheets.
- Hard cap of 2 different loresheets at character creation (Bloodline or "Descendant of" loresheets are not included in this limit).
- Soft cap of 3 dots at CC. Only one loresheet can be at the 3rd dot.

### Flaws
- Every character has 2 points of flaws.
- You can have more than 2, but they won't give you any bonus or extra merit points.

### Advantage Usage (Replenishment)
- Advantages used in-game will be 'greyed out' until replenished.
- They replenish at the rate of one dot per IC night, remaining greyed on the first night after usage.
